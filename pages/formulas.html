All major Electricity & Magnetism formulas with symbol meanings and SI units

Electricity

Electric Laws & Formulas

Topic Formula Variables & Units
Electric Charge Q = ne Q = total charge (C)
n = number of electrons
e = 1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹ C (elementary charge)
Coulomb's Law F = kq₁q₂ / r² F = electrostatic force (N)
k = 8.99 × 10⁹ N·m²/C²
q₁, q₂ = charges (C)
r = distance between charges (m)
Electric Field E = F / q
E = kQ / r² 		E = electric field strength (N/C)
F = force on test charge (N)
q = test charge (C)
Q = source charge (C)
r = distance from source (m)
Ohm's Law V = IR V = voltage / potential difference (V)
I = current (A)
R = resistance (Ω)
Electric Power P = VI P = electric power (W)
V = voltage (V)
I = current (A)
Resistance R = V / I R = resistance (Ω)
V = voltage (V)
I = current (A)
Magnetism

Magnetic Force Formulas

Topic Formula Variables & Units
Magnetic Force on Wire F = BIL sin θ F = magnetic force (N)
B = magnetic field (T)
I = current (A)
L = length of wire (m)
θ = angle between wire and field (°)
Magnetic Force on Charge F = qvB sin θ F = Lorentz force (N)
q = charge (C)
v = velocity of charge (m/s)
B = magnetic field (T)
θ = angle between v and B (°)
