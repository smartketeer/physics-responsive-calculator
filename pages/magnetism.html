Compute magnetic forces on current-carrying wires and moving charged particles

🔗

Magnetic Force on a Wire

F = B × I × L × sin(θ)
💡 Note
Maximum force occurs when θ = 90° (wire perpendicular to field). Force is zero when θ = 0° or 180°.
⚛

Magnetic Force on a Charge

F = q × v × B × sin(θ)
💡 Lorentz Force
This is the Lorentz force law. The direction follows the right-hand rule (for positive charges). The magnitude uses |q|.
