About ElectraMag

ElectraMag is an interactive educational platform designed to help students understand and solve Electricity & Magnetism (E&M) problems. The platform provides accurate formula references, instant interactive calculators, and fully worked sample problems — all with a clean and modern interface. Built as a capstone project for a Physics course, ElectraMag aims to bridge the gap between theory and problem-solving practice.
